**2024–2025 · Research Grant · Wikidata Research Fund administration**

### Context

As part of my role, I handled the administration of Wikimedia Indonesia's Wikidata Research Grant, an annual grant supporting Indonesian researcher teams working on topics related to Wikidata, Wikibase, and open data technology. Over two fiscal years, I managed two grant cycles totaling Rp50,000,000 in research funding.

### What Was Built

In the 2024 fiscal year, I administered a grant for a team from Universitas Indonesia developing FrOG (Framework of Open GraphRAG), an open-source system integrating knowledge graphs with large language models for structured question answering. The team published their findings at [ISRITI 2024 (IEEE)](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10963661){:target="_blank"} and [ESWC LLM-TEXT2KG 2025](https://ceur-ws.org/Vol-4020/Paper_ID_7.pdf){:target="_blank"} in Europe, bringing international visibility to Indonesian open data research.

I collaborated with the researchers team in writing a public summary of their research published in Diff Wikimedia: [Making Question-Answering Systems Smarter with Knowledge Graphs Using FrOG: A Wikidata Research Fund 2024 Highlight](https://diff.wikimedia.org/2025/07/23/making-question-answering-systems-smarter-with-knowledge-graphs-using-frog-a-wikidata-research-fund-2024-highlight/){:target="_blank"}.
In the 2025 fiscal year, I administered a grant for a team from Telkom University working on an agent-based knowledge routing framework, directing user queries to the most appropriate data source using predefined knowledge structures and LLMs. The system achieved strong early results and is currently in the publication process.

### Outcome

Over two grant cycles, the fund supported two Indonesian research teams whose work reached international academic visibility, including publication at IEEE ISRITI 2024 and ESWC LLM-TEXT2KG 2025, and funded research activities at Telkom University.
