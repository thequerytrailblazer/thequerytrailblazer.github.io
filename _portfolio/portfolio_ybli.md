**2026 · NGO · Training Session**

### Context

Yayasan Bina Lentera Insan is a civil society organization whose internal members became the target of this Wikidata training. Reaching an NGO in Manado reflected Wikimedia Indonesia's push to grow open knowledge contributors beyond Western and Central part of Indonesia, bringing structured data literacy to communities in Eastern Indonesia.

### What Was Built

- Sent the teaching team from Wikimedia Indonesia to run an internal WikiLatih Wikidata session for YBLI members via Zoom on 22 January 2026 (13:30 to 15:00)
- Brought the training directly to YBLI's internal staff and members, covering practical Wikidata editing tailored to the organization's context
- Reached 10 participants, 3 staff members, and 1 intern from YBLI

### Outcome

Beyond the training itself, the session introduced YBLI members in Manado to the broader Wikimedia movement. Participants who joined became early contributors to what grew into the Wikimedia Manado Community, turning a single online session into a lasting local presence in the region. As a follow up, in May 2026 we collaborated with Universitas Sam Ratulangi in hosting a WikiLatih events in the Faculty of Agricultural Sciences, engaged over 20 local students and researchers.
