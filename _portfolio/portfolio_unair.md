**2025 · Public University · Students and faculty training session**

### Context

Universitas Airlangga is one of Indonesia's leading state universities, and its Faculty of Science and Technology (FST) represents a key target audience for open data advocacy. Engaging FST UNAIR aligned with Wikimedia Indonesia's goal of embedding open knowledge practices within the next generation of Indonesian researchers and scientists.

### What Was Built

- Arranged and sent a team from the Wikidata Indonesia Community to run WikiLatih Wikidata at the Mathematics Department Laboratory, FST UNAIR Campus Merr, Surabaya on 21 November 2025
- Handled full program execution from trainer assignment to session delivery, covering Wikidata fundamentals and practical applications for science and technology students
- Brought together one trainer and three teaching assistants from the Wikidata Indonesia Community, reaching 20 students from FST UNAIR

### Outcome

The training did more than introduce students to Wikidata, it planted the seed for a local Wikimedia community in Surabaya. Participants who joined became early contributors to what would grow into the Wikimedia Surabaya Community, turning a single training session into a lasting presence in the city.
