**2026 · Government · Training session and researchers networking**

### Context

BRIN is Indonesia's national research and innovation agency, consolidating the country's research institutions under one umbrella. Partnering with BRIN aligned directly with Wikimedia Indonesia's mission to expand open knowledge among the Indonesian research community, particularly around structured data and open publishing.

### What Was Built

- Delivered a hands-on Wikidata editing training (WikiLatih) at BRIN Headquarters, Gedung B.J. Habibie, Jl. MH. Thamrin, running from 09:00 to 13:00 on 3 March 2026
- Training covered foundational Wikidata editing with a focus on Indonesian researchers and BRIN publications, facilitated by Wikimedia Indonesia, Komunitas Wikimedia Jakarta, and Komunitas Wikidata Indonesia
- Reached 31 participants from BRIN staff, supported by a team of 5 trainers and assistants across three organizations
- A signed memorandum of understanding between Wikimedia Indonesia and BRIN

### Outcome

Successfully introduced 31 BRIN researchers to Wikidata editing, contributing to the visibility of Indonesian research output on open knowledge platforms. The session established a working relationship between the Wikimedia researcher community in Indonesia and one of the country's most strategic national research bodies.
