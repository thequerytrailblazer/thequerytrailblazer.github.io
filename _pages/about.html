I am a Data and Technology Partnership Staff at Wikimedia Indonesia, where I focus on expanding the reach and quality of open knowledge infrastructure across Indonesia. I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Science from Universitas Indonesia, where my research centered on foreign language online learning utilizing the Community of Inquiry Framework, work I presented at academic conferences and published in peer-reviewed proceedings.

My engagement with semantic web and knowledge graphs grew through my involvement with the Indonesian Wikimedia community, particularly the Wikidata project. Through this work, I developed institutional partnerships with 20+ universities, government agencies, and cultural institutions across Indonesia, including formal collaborations with BRIN, Kemendikdasmen, and Satu Data Indonesia, and represented Wikimedia Indonesia at international forums including GLAM Wiki 2025 in Lisbon and ESEAP Conference 2026 in Kaohsiung.

I am drawn to the intersection of open data, knowledge infrastructure, and community capacity building, and how these can expand access to knowledge for underrepresented communities and languages.
