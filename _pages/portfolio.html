This portfolio documents my work at Wikimedia Indonesia from 1 September 2023 to 30 June 2026, across two roles: Wikidata Staff and Data and Technology Partnership Staff. It covers partnership programs with academic and civil society institutions, administration of the Wikidata Research Fund, and international conference engagements.

Metrics and documentations

This is a list of institutional documentations hosted by Wikimedia Indonesia which documented my activities and achievements during my time at Wikimedia Indonesia.

Outreach dashboard is a metrics tool used widely by Wikimedia Communities to track volunteers contributions in Wikimedia projects. It was structured per activities (called campaigns), and tracked participants, facilitators, and total contributions in bytes or pages created during the activities.

Wikimedia Indonesia provided reports for every of fiscal year. It consisted of financial statements and also activity documentation. The report is accessible by public.
